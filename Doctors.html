Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to LAU Medical Center's FAQs! We understand that you might have questions about our services, appointments, and more. To ensure a seamless experience, we've compiled a list of commonly asked questions to provide you with quick answers and information. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to our dedicated team for further assistance.

  • What are your operating hours?

    Our medical center is open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

  • How can I schedule an appointment?

    You can schedule an appointment by calling our center directly at +961 1 200800 or by visiting our website and using our online appointment booking system.

  • Do you accept insurance plans?

    Yes, we accept various insurance plans. Please contact our billing department or check our website for a list of accepted insurances.

  • What specialties does your medical center offer?

    We offer a range of specialties including cardiology, pediatrics, orthopedics, obstetrics & gynecology, and more. Visit our website for a complete list.

  • Is there parking available at the medical center?

    Yes, we provide ample parking for patients and visitors in our designated parking area.
